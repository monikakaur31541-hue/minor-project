OUR MISSION

Why We Built
FindIt

Every semester, hundreds of items get lost on campus — phones, wallets, books, keys — and most never make it back to their owners simply because there was no organized system to help.

We saw this problem firsthand and decided to solve it. FindIt was born as an IT department project but quickly became a real service adopted campus-wide. We believe technology should solve everyday problems for real people.

Our goal is simple: if something is lost on campus, FindIt should make sure it gets back to the right person.

HOW IT WORKS

The FindIt Process,
Step by Step

1

Create Your Free Account

Sign up using your college email address. We verify all users are enrolled students to ensure a safe and trusted community. Takes less than 2 minutes.

2

Post a Lost or Found Item

Lost something? Post it with a description, photo, and where you last saw it. Found something? Post where you found it so the owner can claim it. You can add location tags, item category, date, and a description.

3

Smart Matching & Notifications

Our system automatically matches lost and found posts by category and location. When a potential match is detected, both parties get notified instantly via email and in-app alert.

4

Secure In-App Messaging

Communicate directly with the finder or owner through our built-in messaging system. No need to share personal phone numbers — your privacy is protected at all times.

5

Claim & Return

Arrange to collect your item from the campus Lost & Found desk or directly from the finder at a safe public location. Once returned, mark the item as resolved to close the post.
