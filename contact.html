FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is FindIt free to use?
Yes! FindIt is completely free for all enrolled college students. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.
Who can use FindIt?
FindIt is open to all students, faculty, and staff of the college. We verify accounts using college email addresses to maintain a trusted community.
How long do posts stay active?
Lost & Found posts stay active for 30 days by default. After that, they are archived but can be renewed by the poster. Items marked as resolved are removed immediately.
Is my personal information safe?
Absolutely. We never share your phone number or email publicly. All communication goes through our in-app secure messaging system. Your data is stored securely and never sold.
What if someone falsely claims an item?
We ask finders to request identity proof or item description from claimants before handing anything over. You can also report suspicious activity to our team using the report button.
Can I post on behalf of someone else?
Yes, you can. Simply mention in the description that you're posting on behalf of a friend or colleague, and include their contact preference if they consent.
